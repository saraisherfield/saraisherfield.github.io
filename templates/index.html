Who I Am

I am a life long learner and a passionate advocate for safe and sustainable technical solutions. I love baking, gardening, playing video games, coding, reading, thrift shopping, and singing. The tenets of solarpunk philosophy is a guiding principle in my own approach of life. Solarpunk philosophy is in opposition to cyberpunk through the depiction of a brighter future in which technology cultivates community and supports a symbiotic relationship with nature.
